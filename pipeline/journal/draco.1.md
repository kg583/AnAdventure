I ran into Norma yesterday. It would've been a pleasant reunion, a chance to catch up... but there was no time.

I've known something is wrong; Fornax and I haven't seen any other players for a week, which is not unusual for the explorers like Leo & Pyxis, but even Carina's gone missing. She never leaves the village she calls home, and I mean *never*. Even if Orion finally convinced her to join him on some treasure hunt... she wouldn't be gone this long.


Norma confirmed my suspicions, and actually made them worse. She hasn't seen anyone for weeks either: Vela suddenly abandoned her latest dig site, her newest student Vulpecula disappeared after meeting with the Witches, and Corvus... well he's Corvus but even he knows to check in every now and again. There's something in the air, she said, something sinister.

She thinks that something terrible is coming, that wherever our friends went they must not have been safe from it, and that we can't be next. I would trust my teacher with my life, and Fornax trusts in me, so we were ready to drop everything and join her. Surely we would have strength in numbers, I thought,... but she told us no.

She said that she has a mission that's taking her to the distant reaches of the Overworld, and that, as much she'd appreciate our help, she won't bet all of our lives on it. We have no idea how many of us are still out there, and if her plan doesn't work... that could be the end. We need to split up, to ensure at least some of us survive whatever's coming.

So Fornax and I are fleeing to the Nether. It won't be a pleasant living situation, that's for sure, but it may be the safest. We've got some rations, though we'll have to live off Hoglins and Fungi soon enough. We won't be back until we know what we're dealing with.
